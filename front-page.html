What Is Online Casino? Is It Legal?

Online gambling has been around in some form since the mid-1990’s. Initially, the websites were limited in functionality. The popular software developer Microgaming produced the first functioning gambling software in 1994 – this then led to the development of casino online platforms.

As the technology and platforms developed, different commissions and regulatory bodies were created to oversee online gambling. As a result, the legality of online gambling varies greatly from country to country, and in some cases like the USA and Canada, state to state.

Online gambling is legal in many countries such as the UK, France, and Australia. However, in most instances, online casinos are required by law to have a valid gambling license from a governing body like the Malta Gaming Authority.

However, online gambling is also outright banned in many countries too – regardless of a gambling license or not. For example, it is illegal in countries like China, Russia, and Argentina.
